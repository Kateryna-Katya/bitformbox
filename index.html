About

  • Welcome to Battle Roosters - it's basically cockfighting but way more fun and nobody gets hurt. Think WWE but with chickens. Our roosters are back and they're ready to rumble. You can practically hear the crowd going wild when these guys strut into the ring.

Features

  • SECRET SYMBOL

    The Secret symbol is the highest-paying and most elusive icon in the entire game. It appears only in the 7th and final fight of the Fight Spins. You gotta win six fights straight to face the big boss. No pressure, right?

  • BONUS SYMBOL

    In the main game, landing 3 Bonus symbols anywhere on the reels triggers the Fight Spins. Think of these symbols as the bell that starts the fight - ding ding, let's get ready to rumble!
